About Us

MOTOR VEHICLES DEPARTMENT

A) INTRODUCTION:

In order to give effect to the provisions of Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 & Rules made there under, Motor Vehicle Department has been setup as per Section 213 of Motor Vehicle Act 1988 in the State of Maharashtra


B) INTRODUCTION: Vision, Mission, Objectives and Functions of the Department

VISION
-->Safe Transportation of Goods & Passengers
-->Prompt & Citizen friendly services relating to Motor Vehicles

MISSION
-->To aim for stricter compliance of provisions of Motor Vehicle Act & Rules
-->To establish user friendly front end facilities for visiting public
-->To strengthen the Department by providing equipments, manpower & Training
-->To simplify the taxation of vehicles
-->To create effective grievance redressed mechanism

FUNCTIONS
-->To enforce the statutory provisions of Motor Vehicles Act & Rules
-->To collect revenue in the form of Taxes & fees from Motor Vehicles as per the provisions of the law
-->Establishment:The Motor Vehicles Department’s functions are monitored and controlled by Transport Commissioner office, located at Mumbai. The State has 15 Regional Transport Offices and 35 Deputy Regional Transport Offices .The provisions of Motor Vehicle Act are enforced through executive officers, while the ministerial work is carried out by clerical staff.
